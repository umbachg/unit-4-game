Creature Collector

Instructions:

At the beginning of the game you will be given a random number.

There are four creature below. By clicking on a creature you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total exactly to the random number, you lose if your total goes over the random amount.

The value of of each creature is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts the game will change the values of each creature.
